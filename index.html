Why Uruguayan Food?

Uruguayan cuisine is simple, social, and rooted in family. Expect grilled goodness, sweet comforts, and drinks that bring people together.

  • Tradition: Centuries of Mediterranean and local influences.
  • Community: Food is often shared — mate and asado bring people together.
  • Comfort: Sweet and savory treats made by hand.
